History of TechCon

TechCon began in 2010 with a mission to bring together innovators from across the technology landscape. What started as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts has grown into one of the largest technology conferences in the world.

Our mission

TechCon strives to foster innovation, collaboration, and education within the tech community. We aim to provide a platform for sharing cutting-edge developments and creating meaningful connections among professionals and enthusiasts alike.

Notable Past Speakers

Elon Musk

Elon Musk, CEO of SpaceX and Tesla, joined TechCon 2020 to discuss the possibilities of space travel and renewable energy solutions, igniting conversations that are still relevant today.

Sheryl Sanberg

Sheryl Sandberg, COO of Facebook, spoke at TechCon 2018, highlighting the importance of diversity in technology and the role of leadership in driving change within tech organizations.
